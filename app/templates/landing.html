Dual-mode intelligence
Core

Two independent analyses run on every check. Flash Sale fires when the market drops below its historic median by your threshold. Historic Low fires when a price falls below the average of the cheapest 20% ever recorded for that route — regardless of recent trends.

Free tools alert on any dip. {{ app_name }} alerts on significance.

RTW itinerary monitoring
Unique

Monitor a multi-leg itinerary as a single combined price total. Add up to 20 legs, include surface segments for overland connections, and get alerted when the full journey drops by your threshold. No other flight tool does this.

Configurable thresholds
Uncommon

Alert thresholds are set by route type — domestic, regional, and seven international regions — with per-route overrides. Different routes have different normal price swings. You decide what counts as a deal, not us.
